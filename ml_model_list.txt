Purpose
=======

face_detection.py
  Detects an estimated face region from uploaded image bytes.

landmark_detection.py
  Creates face landmark points and landmark ratios used for shape analysis.

face_shape_classifier.py
  Connects face detection, landmarks, and color analysis to produce face shape,
  skin tone, contrast level, symmetry score, and jawline score.

color_analysis.py
  Analyzes image bytes for skin tone, contrast level, brightness, and warmth.

recommendation_engine.py
  Loads trained model/rule files and generates hairstyle, outfit, glasses,
  palette, accessory, and wellness recommendations.

outfit_matcher.py
  Helper functions for outfit recommendation matching.

hairstyle_matcher.py
  Helper functions for hairstyle recommendation matching.

trained_models/face_shape_model.json
  Local model configuration for face shape classification.

trained_models/style_rules.json
  Styling rules, color palettes, and face-shape recommendation data.
